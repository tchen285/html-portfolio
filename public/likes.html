My Top 3 Video Games

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt I have spent more than 180 hours on this game, the DLCs are also amazing. No game can surpass its place in my heart.

The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom I have spent over 190 hours on this game. It has the highest degree of freedom of any game I have ever played.

Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice My first Souls Game. The game process is very challenging, but after completing it, I have endless aftertaste.

Forrest Gump A modern history of the United States. So good!

Green Book A tale of overcoming prejudices, requiring courage.
